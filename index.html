About Us

We are a London-based virtual speed dating platform that aims to provide an alternative to profile swiping through the more personalised speed dating approach.

Our singles community meet one another in short, speedy video calls before deciding if they would like to continue getting to know each other.

We believe it takes more than looking at a person's profile to decide if you would be a good match. We also know our community lead busy lives.
